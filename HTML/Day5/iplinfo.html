About IPL History

Introduction About IPL The Indian Premier League, commonly known as IPL, is one of the most popular T20 cricket leagues in the world. It was started in 2008 by the Board of Control for Cricket in India. The tournament is played every year in India and features top cricket players from different countries.

IPL follows the Twenty20 format, where each team plays 20 overs. The league is famous for its exciting matches, powerful batting, and large fan base. Many talented young players get opportunities to play with international stars through IPL.

Currently, 10 teams participate in the tournament, including famous teams like Chennai Super Kings, Mumbai Indians, and Royal Challengers Bengaluru. The IPL is not only a cricket tournament but also a major entertainment event watched by millions of fans around the world.
